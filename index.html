Our Story

Hot Dogs
Done Right.

Connor's started at a Saturday farmer's market with a beat-up cart, a cooler of all-beef dogs, and a jar of house-made mustard. The line wrapped around the block by noon.

We don't believe in shortcuts. Every sauce is made from scratch. Every dog is fresh. Every single order gets the same love as the first one we ever sold.
